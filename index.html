One Of The Most Endangered

The sumartan rhino is one of the most endangered species on earth. They have lived in Southeast Asia for a millennia. The amount has gone down to fewer than 80 due to habitat loss and poaching. Sumatran rhinos struggle to find a mate and breed.

Did you Know

This species is one of the smallest species of rhinos with there being less than 80 left in the world. The full body leanth of an adult sumatran rhino is 250 cm and it stands 120-142 cm tall. They live up to 40 years.

How Do They Look

The sumartan rhino has gray or brown skin which is very rough. They have large horns which some people hunt them for. They have small eyes and their sense of sight isn't very good.
